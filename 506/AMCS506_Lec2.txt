Air Pollution and Its Dispersion

Air pollution is one of the manmade problems in our
environment

It is an atmospheric condition with various substances are
present at high level of concentration

The concentration of substances become higher enough
which lead to harmful effects in a certain locality

The main poisonous substance are: carbon monoxide (CO),
Nitrogen oxides (NOx), Sulphur di oxide (SO2), suspended
particulate matter (PM-10) and (PM-2.5) etc.

Pollutant dispersion in different urban areas deals with air
turbulence, a complex structure of air flow with different
topography (e.g high-rise buildings, street canyons)

Pollutant dispersion also deals with thermal
effect,
transport and diffusion of heavy gas or dusty particles in
air
